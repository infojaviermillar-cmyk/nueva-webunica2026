# Impeccable Design Context: SolocasasChile

## Target Audience
- **Families in Chile**: Age 25-55, looking to buy or build their own home. They value safety, transparency, and speed. They are often first-time builders.
- **Construction Companies**: B2B segment looking for a platform to showcase their catalog and get qualified leads.

## Use Cases
- Comparing different construction systems (SIP, Prefab, Container).
- Finding a reliable builder with a verifiable "Score de Confianza".
- Managing a digital real estate catalog from a dashboard.

## Brand Personality
- **Performance-Oriented**: High contrast, fast interactions.
- **Reliable/Institutional**: Trust signals, clear typography.
- **Futuristic/Modern LATAM**: Using Indigo and Teal in a way that feels premium, not like a generic SaaS.

## Aesthetic Direction: "Escala Sustentable"
- Use of generous negative space.
- Fluid typography and organic gradients.
- Asymmetry to break the typical "SaaS template" feel.
- High focus on technical precision (Audited quality).
